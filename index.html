    About Koko Mhendo Homestay

    The name, “Koko Mhendo” is derived from a revered flower used by the Tamang tribe of Nepal and India in religious ceremonies, birthdays, and marriages. Just as this flower symbolizes worship and celebration in the Tamang culture, our homestay embodies the warmth, hospitality, and traditions of this vibrant community.
